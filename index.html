About me

# My research interests span Applied Econometrics, Applied Microeconomics, Environmental & Energy Economics, and  Industrial Organization.

# I am the co-organizer of the yearly workshop "Economics of Low-carbon Markets" (the LCM Workshop), which is held since 2013.
